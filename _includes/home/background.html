ABOUT ME

I'm currently living in Springfield, Illinois and work remotely as a full-stack developer for the Medical University of South Carolina, building complex, open-source web applications used to manage costs and services for clinical research studies at universities across the United States. I graduated from College of Charleston in May 2018 with a B.S. in Computer Science and a minor in mathematics.

My goal as a full-stack developer is to provide my customers with beautiful, intuitive front-end solutions supported by a well-structured and thoroughly-tested back-end. Being a developer requires a keen eye for every detail of your work, a drive for innovative thinking and creative problem solving, and the ability to cooperate with others - both providing, and receiving, constructive feedback and criticism.

When I'm not behind the screen, I'm an explorer, musician, and foodie. I love travel the world to experience new places - my favorites so far being Iceland and Switzerland. I've played percussion for more than half of my life, performing with orchestral, jazz, and marching groups. At home, I enjoy trying new recipes in the kitchen and playing with my two wonderful cats.
